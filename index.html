The Stars Above the Whispering Tree

Once upon a time, in a quiet little village where the wind sang lullabies and the moon smiled down gently every night, there was a girl who had the warmest heart in the whole land. Her name was Aya.

She laughed like sunshine and loved with all her heart—especially her father, Loyloy. Loyloy wasn’t just her dad—he was a gentle giant of kindness, with eyes like calm lakes and a voice that made even storms quiet down.

Every night before bed, he would tell Aya a story. He told her about the Whispering Tree, a glowing tree that grew on the highest hill and held the dreams of every child in the village.

“One day,” he told her, “if you ever feel lost or lonely, just close your eyes and go to the Whispering Tree. I’ll be there, waiting.”

One day, Loyloy had to leave—not to another village, but to a place beyond the clouds. Aya was heartbroken. The world seemed quieter, like it had lost its music.

That night, when she finally fell asleep, she dreamed. In her dream, she walked barefoot under a glowing sky. The stars above whispered her name. There stood the Whispering Tree—and under it, her father.

“Papa?” she whispered. Loyloy smiled, arms wide. “You found me, just like I said.”

“I miss you,” she said. “I miss you too,” he replied, hugging her. “But I’m always with you. Just look up at the stars—that’s where I’ll be.”

From that night on, every time she closed her eyes, the stars danced a little brighter. And under the Whispering Tree, her father Loyloy was always waiting.

Made with love 💛 for those we never stop missing.
